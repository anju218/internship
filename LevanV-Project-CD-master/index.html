Role of School and College Magazines in Education

Magazines published from Schools and Colleges have great importance in education. These magazines often reflect the image of the educational institution through the articles presented by the students and teachers. School and College magazines are great way to encourage the creativity in students which makes them effectively utilize language for conveying ideas. The true potential in writing among students can only be recognized through the work in magazines because there will not be much exposure in other ways.

School and College magazines are the most appropriate platform for students for exposure to the artistic potential among peer. Most of the budding talents in school, gives opportunity to showcase their work through school magazines. The articles published in the school magazine also allows teachers to motivate the young readers. Teachers can also help students to improve their language by giving proper suggestions and encouragement. As there are different kind of literature being published on school magazines, students can choose categories based on their interest for publishing in them. It also helps the students to know about inter and intra college news to expand their connectivity and ideas to explore beyond the clouds. This will aid the students to do research on topics on their own choice of interest.

Why Digital Magazines?

Usually magazines are available in two formats

  • Digital format
  • Printed format

Printed magazine has started to decline in popularity and diffusion because of increasingly advantages of digital magazines as the people are tending to digitalization. Some of them includes:

• Lower cost: A paper magazine has to be printed, distributed and finally sold. These three processes, incur a lot of cost. Digital, on the contrary, allows publishers to distribute their magazines on the web, without having to pay the commercial mediation of suppliers and sellers.

• Distribution: There is no need to spend money on distributors and every issue of the magazine can be potentially bought by anyone in the world.

• Access for the reader: The reader can get the magazine anywhere and at anytime.

• Virality: This era has brought new ways to for the users to connect their audience through social media sites. This can increase ease of sharing it anyone.

• Interaction: Through these social media sites, users can also contact with the company through comments, likes and private messages. This would be a great chance to listen to the audience feedback to improve in the next volume.

Advertisements in Magazine

Readers report that they are most influenced by magazines than any other media.Magazines are more trusted, inspiring and motivating than other media among young adults. Almost 30% of the world reads magazines. By using magazines as a medium for advertising, an appeal can be made to a large body of persons with similar tastes or covering a large area.

• Targeted Reach: Different magazines will have different audience. Through magazine advertisements, we can find the right magazine for the right audience to place the ads strategically.

• Brand Recognition and Credibility: Many magazines are well-respected among their targeted audience usually the level of trust is elevated. Advertisers can greatly benefit from this credibility when their ads are viewed not as ads but as recommendations from a trusted source.

• Pass-on Readership: Many people pass on magazines to their friends and family members. This likely increases the reach of the advertisement to more potential customers.
